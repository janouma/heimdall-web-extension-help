loging out of the extension

If you wish to disconnect your Heimdall account from the extension, open the extension settings page

  • on mac os: Safari > Settings > Extensions > Heimdall widget > Settings
  • on ios: Settings > Safari > Extensions > Heimdall widget > Extension settings

then simply click on your user name, the disconnection will happen and the login form will show up again.
